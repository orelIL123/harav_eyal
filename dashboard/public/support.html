Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in to the app?

You can log in using your phone number or Google account. Tap "Login" on the main screen and follow the instructions.

How can I watch lessons?

All lessons are available on the home screen and in the lessons library. Tap on any lesson to start watching.

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications and enable the notifications you want. You'll receive updates about new lessons and content.

The app is not loading / frozen

Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the problem persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

How do I report a problem?

Send us a message via WhatsApp or email with a description of the problem and a screenshot if possible. We'll get back to you soon.

Is the app free?

Yes! The app and its content are free for everyone.

Response Times

Our support team is available Sunday-Thursday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Israel time). We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours.

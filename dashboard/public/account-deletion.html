Account and Data Deletion

Rabbi Eyal Amrami App

Two Ways to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

The fastest and easiest way to delete your account is directly through the app:

  1. Open the Rabbi Eyal Amrami app
  2. Tap "My Profile" in the bottom menu
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and data will be deleted immediately and permanently.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can send a deletion request by email:

Send an email to:

contact@eyalamrami.com

Include the following information in your email:

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Body:
Hello,

I would like to delete my account and data from the Rabbi Eyal Amrami app.

My email address: [Enter your email address]

Thank you

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email when the deletion is complete.

锔 Important - Permanent Deletion!

Account deletion is irreversible and will include:

  • Deletion of your account details
  • Deletion of viewing and usage history
  • Deletion of preferences and personalized settings
  • Deletion of all data associated with your account

You will not be able to recover your account or data after deletion.

What Happens to My Information?

After account deletion:

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems
  • You will not be able to log in to the app with this account
  • All notifications and updates will be stopped
  • Anonymous statistical data may be retained for general analysis purposes (without personal identification)

Processing Time

  • Deletion via app: Immediate
  • Email request: Up to 30 days

More Questions?

For any questions or issues with the deletion process, contact us:
